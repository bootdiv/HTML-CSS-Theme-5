Viewpoint

We believe helping is a privilege. We believe that people and environments can be helped and that helping is an activity which when engaged in raises the quality of life it touches, helper and helped alike. We believe in helping ourselves and our environments to do well in life through gaining knowledge and by providing ideas, products and services which truly make a difference for the better to the quality of life affected by those ideas, that knowledge and those products and services.

is a team in action. We accumulate wealth by supporting, investing in and acquiring ownership of companies that either already are or potentially will flourish and prosper through providing ideas, products and delivering services which increase the well-being of others and/or the environments we live in.

We do this by either purchasing the company's stock, or sometimes by investing capital in the venture and thereby gaining ownership in that venture or a profitable return on our investment within a specified time or both. We do our due diligence in regard to determining the management capability of those responsible for the company we are considering investing in or the management capability of the individual(s) responsible for the venture.

We examine their capability to ensure the company/venture will deliver what it promises to the satisfaction of its customers/clients and the management’s capability to establish and expand a viable and prosperous company/venture for its investors to benefit from. People and environments helped through ideas, products and services which are truly beneficial are what we strive to bring about.
