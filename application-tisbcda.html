Trainee Investment Specialist in
Blockchain and Crypto Digital Assets:

  • Utilize research techniques, data collection techniques and analysis technology.
  • A Specialist employs problem-solving skills, data interpretation, and various other tools.
  • Align the use of investment methodologies with profit goals.
  • Understand and effectively predicts a given asset’s value and behavioral outlook.
  • Quantitatively analyze an asset’s statistical data, identify trends and patterns in relation to recent and longer term activity in order to help MPI acquire assets.
  • Provide portfolio managers with relevant data that helps them to make better-informed investment decisions.
  • Write up reports, present data to management and other team members, make recommendations when required.
  • Work with trading strategies that help manage portfolio risk.
  • Work with methodologies which protect databases and confidential information.
  • Monitor projects and make sure they are completed in a timely manner.
  • Must be able to deliver presentations and communicate effectively when appropriate.
